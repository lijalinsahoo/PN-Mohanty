About Dr. P.N. Mohanty

Dr. P.N. Mohanty is a name synonymous with in fertility treatment in Odisha .Since the last 36 years she has been serving society with her expertise in Obs & Gynae services .Calm poised and full of life , Dr Mohanty knowns how to make her patients feel comfortable by her healing gestures and care . While growing up ,she never wanted to be a doctor ,but after receiving degrees from the ace institutes of India ,she felt that she was made for this job .She realized that by elevating people from their physical and mental suffering ,she could give them peace of mind .From then onwords bringing a smile on the face of every patient ,became the sole purpose of her life .

She completed graduation from SCB Medical College and post graduation from PGI Chandigarh .She joined Ispat General Hospital , Rourkela .After Service it for 19 years as the head of the Department and senior Deputy Director ,She decided to come back to her soil and work for the people in Bhubanewar .’’I observe that there was a lack proper infertility treatment facilities in this city in the city, but there was a lot of demand for IVF treatment ,so I wanted to serve people in this city which is also my home .’’

The uniqueness the profession motivated her to pursue the service further .Finally she started his own clinic “Swarnashanti “named after the two most influential women of her life ,her mother and mother -in -law .The centre has all the facilities for IVF treatment in Bhubsneswar .Speaking further about choosing a clinic for IVF treatment she advices that couple should check the success stories of the clinic and the attitude of doctors.

She has brought a bundle of joy in the lives of many childless couples through her treatment .Most of her patients have been ladies above 45 years of age .Sharing her views on the myths that are still prevalent in the society she says ,”most the times women are held responsible for not being able to conceive. In some cases if the woman suffering from the hormonal imbalance due to days functional ovaries ,then it is assumed that some body had tied her ovaries and is hence unable to conceive.’’

Through Assisted Reproductive Technologies have proved to be a boon for many couples ,its come with its own share for risk ,that mainly lead to OHSS and multiple pregnancies ,informs the doctor. She further adds that during the process of ovum pick up that is done by needle ,there are possibilities of vessels being ruptured’’. She has observe that it is mainly the treatment cost that discourage the patients from undergoing ART services.

She believes treating people is more than just a profession for her ,’’I make a lot of monetary concession for the patients. I put my heart and soul along with my knowledge in their treatment ,’’she quips with a smile .But there are tough times when issues of ‘’Unexplained fertility ‘’crops us .In such cases she takes up the challenges of carrying out the procedure more than once ,while asking the patient to maintain a positive frame of mind .Meanwhile ,she also inform of couple about the limitation of the treatment .’’I ensure that the pregnancy become as important for me, as it to do them’’ adds the doctor .

While most of the time she is found serving her patients , she also love socializing ,surfing the net ,doing household chores and writing poems in her free time .’’I used to write many love letter for my friends in my college days’’ conclude the doctor with twinkle in her eye.
